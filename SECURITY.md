# Security Policy

## Supported versions

Enginery has not yet published a stable release. Security reports are accepted
against the `main` branch until the first tagged release. This policy will
list supported release lines once `v0.1.0` ships.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Report suspected vulnerabilities privately through GitHub's [private
vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/Mathews-Tom/Enginery/security/advisories/new)
for this repository. Do not open a public issue for a suspected
vulnerability.

Include:

- the affected version or commit;
- reproduction steps or a proof of concept;
- the observed impact.

Expect an initial response within five business days. Enginery is currently
maintained by a single owner during early development; response times may
vary outside that window.
